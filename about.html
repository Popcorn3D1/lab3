About This Site


Piles Of Garbage By The Shore from Pexels.


This site addresses plastic pollution, a widespread environmental problem harming marine life, ecosystems, and human health. It explores how the overuse of plastics and poor waste management have resulted in vast accumulations of plastic waste in landfills, oceans, and the natural world.
